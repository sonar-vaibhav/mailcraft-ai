📖 How to Use Mail Craft

1

🤖 Craft It Button

When composing an email in Gmail, you'll see a "Craft It" button below the compose box.

Click it to get AI-powered tone options:

  • 👔 Formal - Professional and business-like
  • 😊 Friendly - Warm and approachable
  • 📝 Concise - Short and to the point
  • 💪 Persuasive - Compelling and convincing
  • 🎯 Custom - Your own instructions
2

⚡ Quick Shortcut: !@#

Type !@# at the end of your email, followed by your custom instruction.

Example:
Hi John, I need to reschedule our meeting. !@# Make this more polite and professional

Press Shift + # to trigger the AI rewrite with your custom prompt!

3

✨ What Happens Next

Mail Craft will:

  • Read your email content (subject + body)
  • Send it to Google Gemini AI
  • Rewrite it according to your chosen tone
  • Replace the content in your compose box
  • Show a success notification

🎯

📝

🔄

Try Different Tones

Experiment with different tones to find what works best for your audience

⚡
